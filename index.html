F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of loans can your platform manage?

Our platform supports a wide range of loan products, including personal loans, business loans, commodity loans, and embedded financing, with full automation from origination to repayment.

How does your AI-driven underwriting work?

We use AI algorithms that assess creditworthiness using both traditional and alternative data sources. This enables us to evaluate borrowers with limited or no credit history accurately and quickly.

Can financial institutions integrate this with their existing systems?

Yes. Our platform is built with open APIs and modular architecture, making integration with core banking systems, CRMs, and third-party platforms seamless.

Is this platform suitable for small or growing lenders?

Absolutely. Whether you're a microfinance institution, cooperative, or a scaling fintech, our solution is built for flexibility and growth — start small and scale as you expand.

What is digital KYC and how secure is it?

Digital KYC (Know Your Customer) is a paperless onboarding process that verifies user identity using secure digital methods. All customer data is encrypted and complies with leading data protection standards.

Do you support recurring payments?

Yes. Our system allows both one-time and automated recurring payments, including ACH and mobile payments, making collections efficient and reliable.
